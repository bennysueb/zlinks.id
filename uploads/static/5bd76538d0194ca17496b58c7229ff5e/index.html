about AIGIS

Annual Indonesia Green Industry Summit

An annual event organised by the Ministry of Industry of the Republic of Indonesia to promote industrial transformation towards greener and more sustainable practices. AIGIS serves as a platform to share knowledge, experiences, and innovations in an effort to achieve sustainable economic growth through green industry.

Agus Gumiwang Kartasasmita, Minister of Industry

"Currently, the domestic industry faces challenges in terms of importation and availability of recycled raw materials. Through AIGIS 2025, innovative solutions are expected to be found to increase the utilisation of local raw materials and strengthen the domestic supply chain."

Faisol Riza, Deputy Minister for Industry

"Industrial transformation towards green industry is no longer an option, but a necessity for the future of our nation and the Earth"

Green Golf 2025 is an exclusive golf tournament designed to promote sustainability with a focus on carbon neutrality and green innovation. More than just an environmentally-friendly golf event, it strengthens friendships and fosters collaboration among industry leaders, investors, and policymakers. This event integrates sustainable practices to support the transition towards a greener future for the industry. Green Run 2025

Join us for the pre-event of The AIGIS 2nd 2025, where sports meet sustainability. Green Run 2025 features three exciting categories: 5K Green Race, 5K Fun Run, and 10K Green Run. This event is built on the principles of carbon neutral and zero waste, with eco-friendly initiatives like minimizing plastic waste, water refill stations, and a carbon offset mechanism. More than just a race, it's a call to action for environmental change!

The 2nd AIGIS 2025

This year, the Ministry of Industry proudly presents the 2nd Annual Indonesia Green Industry Summit (AIGIS 2nd) 2025. The summit is dedicated to supporting Indonesia’s ambitious goal of achieving net-zero emissions by 2060, with a strong focus on accelerating the decarbonization of the industrial sector to meet its target of net-zero emissions by 2050.

Be part of this transformative event and contribute to shaping a greener, more sustainable industrial future.
